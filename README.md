# Superstore Sales Analysis using MySQL

## Project Overview
Analyzed 9,996 orders from a US E-Commerce company 
to find sales trends, profitable categories, 
and loss-making products using MySQL.

## Tools Used
- MySQL Workbench
- Excel (for initial data view)

## Dataset
- Source: Kaggle (Sample Superstore Dataset)
- Rows: 9,996 orders
- Columns: 21 features

## Key Findings
1. Total Revenue = $2,272,449
2. West region has highest sales = $713,471
3. Technology is most profitable category = $145,387
4. Sean Miller is top customer = $25,043 in sales
5. Cubify 3D Printer is biggest loss product = -$8,879
6. Company grew 50% from 2014 to 2017
7. Discounts above 20% are causing losses
8. 5 out of top 10 states are making losses

## Business Recommendations
1. Cap discounts at maximum 20%
2. Investigate pricing in Texas and Ohio
3. Review Cubify 3D Printer pricing strategy
4. Focus more resources on West and East regions
